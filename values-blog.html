Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

An ethical decision I have had to make recently was to provide an alternative platform for people to listen to my music. Recently news broke that the ceo of the largest music streaming company Spotify was using the millions earned off of the artists on the platform to invest in Ai drone research to be used in warfare. After a great deal of public outcry many musicians are considering alternative platforms to host and release thier music with some removing their music from the platform entirely. While I have a very small audience and do not believe that my personal catalogue is generating much if any money that could be spent on warmongering and things my values do not align with whatsoever, I did want to provide the people within my community who do engage with my music an alternative cost free way to download and listen. I made a small html website that hosts a link to a file where anyone can download all of my released music for free, as well as a small version of a streaming site for the songs so people can listen online. It is important to me that music and art can be free and accessible for everyone and being in control of my own method of providing that seemed like the best choice.

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

I have been deeply influenced by local art collectives and communities in Te Whanganui-a-Tara, the way in which people come together to make events and collaborative projects happen has shaped what I think is possible with limited resources.

Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

I am a persistent learner, I am able to self manage my time and learning well and I am a clear communicator in group settings. I think my ability to work well with others will be very valuable in this learning journey, especially given the often collaborative nature of coding and software development.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

I can struggle with engaging with topics that I dont have a personal interest in which can be detrimental to my learning when not kept in check.
